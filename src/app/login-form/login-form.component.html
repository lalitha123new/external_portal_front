Neuropathology Lab

The Neuropathology Lab offers comprehensive diagnosis for brain tumors, epilepsy, neuromuscular disorders, neuroinfections, neurometabolic & neurodegenerative disorders including advanced molecular diagnosis, genetic tests & electron microscopy.
